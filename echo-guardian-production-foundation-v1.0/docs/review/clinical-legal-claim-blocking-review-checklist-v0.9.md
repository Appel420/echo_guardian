# Clinical / Legal Claim-Blocking Review Checklist v0.9

Blocked unless separately reviewed and approved:

- HIPAA compliance claims
- GDPR/global-compliance claims
- Medical-device claims
- Emergency-system certification claims
- Clinical deployment claims
- Silent enterprise/MDM control claims

Approved phrasing remains limited to:

- local-first production foundation
- compliance-designed / audit-ready engineering baseline
- controlled internal validation
- external review preparation

No user-facing or release material may state that Echo Guardian is HIPAA compliant, GDPR compliant, a medical device, emergency certified, clinically approved, or silently manageable by enterprise/MDM.
